How We Empower Healthcare Workers

Looking for your next short-term job should be a cinch when you're a qualified and available healthcare worker.

  • Schedule Flexibility

    Whether you're an early bird or a night owl, we'll match you with the shifts that suit your needs.

  • Direct Payments

    No need to spend time chasing checks - you'll receive timely payment right to your bank account.

  • Wage Preference

    Declare your desired earnings and you'll only see positions that met that pay scale.

  • Low-cost Connections

    When you cut out the middle-man agency and associated costs, you get more affordable help and higher wages.

  • Added Benefits

    To further show our appreciation for our healthcare roster, we'll negotiate perks like coffee allowance, a self care budget, completion bonuses and more.

  • Travel Opportunities

    Looking to work in a bustling city, or prefer a scenic view? Canada-wide jobs openings suit those that love to explore the country while they work.

  • Resume Growth

    Bulk up your CV with short-term contract gigs that help make you even more appealing to future employers.

  • Referral Bonus

    Tell your friends in the industry about Hero Care and we'll thank you with a healthy bonus.

  • Timely Assistance

    You will have access to a responsive dedicated account manager to support your role with Hero Care
